Self Quote Chain Technique

Source Tweet Id: 2014167228024021283
Source Handle: seisei_ai_1st
Technique Description: Self-quote chain (セルフ引用で数珠繋ぎ) — instead of posting a new tweet, quote your own previous tweet. This creates a chain where each new post carries the engagement signal of the previous one. The algorithm sees the quoted post's metrics and gives the new post a boost. Called 'doubling impressions without reducing posts' (投稿減らさずにインプ2倍).
Strategic Value: High. This directly addresses the reported algorithm change where X supposedly wants fewer posts but higher quality. Self-quote chains let you maintain posting volume while the algorithm treats it as higher-quality engagement signals.
Risk Level: Low. This is not a TOS violation — self-quoting is normal X behavior. The risk is that X may eventually discount self-quotes, but for now it appears to work.
Applicability: Both EN and JP accounts. Particularly valuable for the image chain format — post a set of related images as a self-quote chain so the audience sees the whole series.
